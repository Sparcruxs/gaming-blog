Guild Wars 2

Guild Wars 2 is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed by ArenaNet and published by NCSOFT. It features a dynamic event-driven quest system and action-oriented combat.


Key Features:

  • Dynamic event-driven quest system
  • Action-oriented combat
  • Expansive world with diverse environments
  • Regular updates and expansions
  • Strong community and social features
