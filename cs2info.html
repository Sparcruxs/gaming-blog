Counter-Strike 2

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) is the next-gen upgrade of the legendary tactical shooter by Valve. Built on the Source 2 engine, it features improved graphics, physics, and gameplay while staying true to CS:GO’s competitive core.

Two teams—Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists—battle in strategic matches where precision and teamwork are key. Whether you're a casual player or aiming for esports, CS2 delivers intense and rewarding gameplay.

Key Features:

  • Enhanced graphics and physics with the Source 2 engine
  • New maps and updated classic maps
  • Improved matchmaking and anti-cheat systems
  • New weapons and equipment
  • Customizable player skins and cosmetics
